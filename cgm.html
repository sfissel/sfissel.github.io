Continuous Glucose Monitoring (CGM)

TEAMWORK ● PROJECT MANAGEMENT ● MISSING DATA IMPUTATION ● TIME SERIES ANALYSIS

S u m m a r y

For my Master's Program Capstone Project, I am currenly working in a four-person team seeking to advance our understanding of CGM data analysis, with a focus on the implications of missing data and the potential to derive meaningful insights in the presence of such data challenges. Additionally, we aim to determine an effective way to impute the missing data.
Overall, our research aims to contribute to the optimization of diabetes monitoring and treatment strategies by leveraging CGM data effectively, despite its inherent data gaps, and ensuring the reliability of our findings through robust imputation methods.
