Databases: Discussing Popular Architectures

TEAMWORK ● DATABASES ● MYSQL ● NEO4J ● MONGODB ● PYTHON

S u m m a r y

During the Forge Launch Academy, I worked in a four-person team to design a custom dataset, populate it into MySQL, MongoDB, and Neo4j, write Python scripts to query each of them, and examine the strengths and weaknesses of each database architecture. We then collectively wrote a Medium article explaining what we learned about each database and also presented our custom dataset and findings.
